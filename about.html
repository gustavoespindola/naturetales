About Us

We are creating new experiences using our vibroacoustic floor and specially produced music as we turn self-discovery into a playful journey towards wellness. The Vibroacoustic Studio is a comfortable, cozy and safe space hosting contemplative, transformative and celebrational events.

Our Team

Ishtadi

Our passionate Ishtadi is the founder of Nature Tales and a highly experienced yoga, meditation, breathing and relaxation teacher as well as a sound therapist and a stress coach. He had lived in India and studied with monks, nuns, siddhas, yogis, musicians and a variety of therapists and healers. He is a music producer and one of our resident sound artists. His current work is directed towards bridging music and movement cultures, focusing on the aspect of mindfulness and immersion into the now. He facilitates, produces, improvises and mixes music to ceremonies, parties, concerts, wellness, dance and relaxation experiences. He also takes part of our technical team and the event production team.

Respira

Our peaceful Respira is the co-founder of Nature Tales, one of our resident sound artists and a music producer. He is passionate about soundscapes of free form and fluidity, exploring texture and depth. Playing with the contrasts and similarities of organic and electronic sound, he aims towards union and coherence between nature and technology. He is the curator of Nature Tales Label in charge of our immersive weekly music releases and the artist behind our graphic design.

Kertu

Our eloquent Kertu is a spiritual seeker with a marketing background. She envisions a New Earth, and is therefore passionate about helping value-driven new paradigm businesses to spread their message. She is the one who does magic with words through all our social media channels, inviting you to get to know what we do. She shares our audiovisual content with tons of love and keeps us in close touch with all members of our community.

Cami

Our powerful Cami came all the way from Chile to make things real. With experience in film and event production, collaborative organization and creative problem solving, she uses her skills in communication, teamwork and critical thinking to plan, organize and coordinate our teams and collaborators, helping us all to maintain focus and going forward in this collective effort that is Nature Tales Studio. She also takes part in the event production team.

Lasse

Our direct Lasse Mol is the one who plans and develops our friendly business model. He has a wide background, from care-taking kids and people with special needs to theater production, event productions and sustainable farming. He has recently developed a big interest in understanding economy and businesses, as he wants to back the great projects and ideas of those he holds dear.

Aitor

Aitor is the man behind the visual content of the studio. Coming from a photography background, lately he is diving into the storytelling potential of videography. With a camera in hand, he gives shape to the vision of The Vibroacoustic Studio using photos and videos. His objective is not only to communicate the innovative approach of the studio, but also to spread knowledge about healthy physical and mental lifestyle based in conscious movement and mindfulness.
